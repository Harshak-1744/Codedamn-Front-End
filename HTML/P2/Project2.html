Preparation Steps

Chana Masala (Chickpea Curry)

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight.
  2. Cook with tomatoes, onions, garlic, and spices until tender.
  3. Serve hot with rice or bread.

Aloo Gobi (Potato and Cauliflower Curry)

  1. Stir-fry potatoes and cauliflower with turmeric, cumin, and coriander until golden.
  2. Add water and simmer until tender.
  3. Adjust seasoning and serve.

Palak Paneer (Spinach and Cottage Cheese)

  1. Blanch spinach and grind it into a paste.
  2. Cook with paneer, garlic, garam masala, and cream.
  3. Adjust seasoning and serve hot.

Dal Tadka (Lentil Curry)

  1. Cook lentils until soft.
  2. Prepare a tadka (tempering) with mustard seeds, cumin, garlic, and chili in hot oil.
  3. Pour the tadka over the cooked lentils, mix well, and serve.
